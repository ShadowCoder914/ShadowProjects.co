How it all began


ShadowProjectsCreativeStudios was created as a Software & Art organization. It's founder Shamari Hankins, has always prided himself on pushing for excellence in everything that he does. Born in Far Rockaway-New York in the Queens area; He grew up in middle-class homes for most of his life. He attended elementary and middle schools early on in New York, and did some years in junior-high in the state of Georgia. This was due to rising inflations, and a flourishing yet struggling mother and father due to the economy's inflated pricing and conditions. Shamari then ; would go on to finish high-school in New york state. He mentions he was always into technology and the creative arts ever since a mere child.
He recalls and attributes most if not all of his drive and determination to growing up playing sports, being very athletic and motivated, and always pushing to challenge himself.
